Sorry, it seems like you're not on the bus yet.

While it's so great that you're excited to share your feelings, we ask that you wait until you've boarded!




This project is an iteration of a geographic emotional tracker that aims to map the sentiments of passengers along various bus, train, and plane routes across the United States and the world. It was created by Roopa Vasudevan.

A companion map-based visualization of collected sentiments will soon be accessible from this site, and will be exhibited as part of Fung Wah Biennial, a 2016 Major Exhibition at Flux Factory.
